#### General Information
Seford Stronghold is the primary training ground for the [[Cinderwatch]], and also serves as the primary residency for many high ranking military officials.  The name accounts for the entire military installation, despite the fact that the actual castle fortress does not encompass the entire base.  

#### Important Locations
- The Stronghold — the actual castle located at the center of the base, the Stronghold is the traditionally used as a storage reserve of large military equipment (Ballistas, Battering Rams, Etc.).  Small scale weaponry (swords, bows, etc.) and armor is also stored here.
- The Barracks — home to the troops that stay on base, the barracks are where most of the members who stay on base live
- The Pyre — the base training ground; new recruits are often seen here on training exercises
- Stonehaven — acts as the officer headquarters; the upper levels are dedicated to various military exercises, while the lower levels are exist more as a social area for troops; the primary pub/tavern for the troops is located on the ground floor.
#### Political Affiliation/Status
As the primary military force of the empire, which serves the crown directly, it is safe to say that the members of the [[Cinderwatch]] are extremely loyal to the crown, and would not take dissent against the crown lightly.
